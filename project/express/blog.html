Points of Interest enroute to and in Whistler

By Richard Smith, Nov 15

Enjoy the drive of a lifetime along the Sea to Sky Highway to Whistler. It's one of the most scenic routes in the world. Besides the world renowned winter skiing and snow sports, Whistler offers an abundance of sights and activities throughout the year - hiking and mountain biking trails, river rafting, ATV off-roading, trout fishing, zip-lining, and don’t forget the Audain Art Museum and Squamish Lil’wat Cultural Center.
